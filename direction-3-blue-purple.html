A Message From

Message from Award Program Chair and APSIC President

The APSIC Environmental Hygiene Excellence Award has been established to shine a light on the healthcare facilities across Asia Pacific that go above and beyond in maintaining safe, clean, and infection-free environments for their patients and staff.


Environmental hygiene is the unsung hero of infection prevention — a meticulous discipline that requires commitment, expertise, and continuous improvement. This Award celebrates the teams and institutions who have made that commitment, and whose work has directly contributed to saving lives.


We believe this recognition will inspire others across the region to raise their standards, to innovate, and to champion hygiene excellence as a core value of quality healthcare. We warmly invite eligible institutions to participate in the 2025 Award programme.

FAQ

Have a question?
We are here to answer.

Who is eligible to participate?+
Any accredited healthcare facility operating within the Asia Pacific region is eligible to apply, including general hospitals, specialty centres, and long-term care facilities. The facility must be able to demonstrate active environmental hygiene programmes with supporting documentation.
What is the objective of the Award Programme?+
The AEHEA aims to identify, recognise, and celebrate healthcare facilities across Asia Pacific that have demonstrated outstanding commitment to environmental hygiene and infection prevention. The programme also serves to surface and share best practices that can inspire improvement across the region.
Is there a participation fee?+
No. The AEHEA application and evaluation process is entirely free of charge for all participating institutions. There are no submission, processing, or ceremony attendance fees.
What are the evaluation criteria?+
Applications are evaluated across three core criteria: Commitment to IPC (sustained organisational focus on infection prevention), Innovative Practices (evidence-based improvements to hygiene protocols), and Quality of Care (measurable impact of hygiene practices on patient safety outcomes).
How are finalists selected?+
All applications undergo a structured, blinded peer-review process conducted by APSIC's expert jury panel. Applications are scored against standardised rubrics, and finalists are selected based on the strength of their evidence and overall score. All applicants receive written feedback.
Will additional information be kept confidential?+
Yes. All submission materials and facility data shared as part of the application process are treated as strictly confidential. Information is only accessed by APSIC jury members for evaluation purposes and is never shared with third parties without explicit consent.
