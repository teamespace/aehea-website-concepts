The Project

A prestige award website
for hospitals across Asia Pacific

🏥 What is AEHEA?

The APSIC Environmental Hygiene Excellence Award — recognises hospitals that demonstrate excellence in environmental hygiene and patient safety. Scientifically led by APSIC, delivered in cooperation with Schülke.

🎯 Primary Objective

Signal prestige and credibility. The site must convey scientific authority and a premium, trustworthy presence so the award is taken seriously across Asia Pacific. Driving hospital applications is the secondary goal.

👤 Audience

Hospitals, IPC (Infection Prevention & Control) specialists, and quality leads across Asia Pacific. English-speaking professional audience. Tone: scientific authority meets premium, clean, clinical design.

⚠️ Non-Negotiable Rule

APSIC leads. Always. Schülke is a cooperation partner — never the owner or judge. Nothing in design or copy may imply Schülke influences judging. Scientific independence must be preserved at all times.

For Your Elux Brief

Key rules Elux
must not miss

🏛️ APSIC leads at all times

APSIC / AEHEA brand every page. Logos appear together only in the "Partners in safer care" section. Schülke's home is the Cooperation Partner feature on Winners & Partners page.

🎨 Direction 2 gets Schülke assets

The Schülke 'plus' arrow device and halftone blue gradations (40/20/10%) are Direction 2 only. Directions 1 and 3 use only subtle cues — rounded corners. No arrow, no halftone.

📱 8-step flow = mobile-first

The interactive application timeline on "How to Participate" must work cleanly on mobile. This is called out specifically in the brief — it's a design challenge Elux must solve, not an afterthought.

📐 Structure is sacred

Directions change look & feel only — not the sitemap, not the wireframe layout, not the copy. Elux follows the approved wireframe structure exactly. No reordering, no adding sections.

🔤 Typography rules

Body copy: Open Sans across all three directions. Headers: Google fonts — propose specific faces per direction. Direction 3 should use a serif or distinctive display face to differentiate.

🖼️ Imagery: Elux to source

Clean, minimalist, studio-quality medical/clinical environments. Advanced environmental hygiene — calm, premium, no visual clutter. Tailored per direction.
